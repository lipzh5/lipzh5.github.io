Current Research Topics
  • Realistic Humanoid Facial Expression Imitation
  • Nuanced Humanoid Facial Expression Generation
  • Affective Human-Robot Interaction System

I am a PhD candidate at the School of Computing of Macquarie University, advised by Longbing Cao. My research focuses on humanoid facial expression imitation and generation, as well as how humanoid robots can communicate affectively with humans. Before joining Stanford, I earned both my master's and bachelor's degrees from Sun Yat-sen University and gained 3 years of experience as an Senior Game Development Engineer at NetEase Games.
